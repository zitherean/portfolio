I’m a quick learner, a team player, and I enjoy solving problems with others. Outside of tech, I’m really into music and football. This site showcases some of my work. Feel free to connect!

Projects

FM for Ubuntu (C++)

A compact, terminal-based football management game for two players. Designed as a personal exploration of probability and statistics in simulation games, FM for Ubuntu blends strategic choices with chance-driven outcomes to create competitive match experiences.

Blackjack (C++)

A terminal-based Blackjack game supporting up to 7 players. Built to simulate core gameplay mechanics like hit, stand, split, and double down, this project explores the decision-making logic and flow of turn-based card games in a local multiplayer setting.
